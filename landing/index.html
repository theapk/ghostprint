How this works

    
Three steps. Nobody can see the stamp. The original maker can prove it's theirs later.


    

      

        
1

        
You drop in your file

        
Any STL, up to 50 MB. We don't change how it looks, prints, or feels in the slicer.

      


      

        
2

        
We stamp two invisible marks

        
One rides along inside the print instructions. One hides in the shape itself, smaller than your nozzle can see. Both encode the order ID, printer, and time.

      


      

        
3

        
Anyone can check it later

        
If a knockoff shows up, drop the original STL back in and we'll tell you whether the part matches. Takes a few seconds. Works offline.
